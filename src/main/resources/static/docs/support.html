Support and contribution

Get involved with Looper development by reporting any bug(s) or request for new feature(s) or page(s), and something else. Just fill our support form, please logged in to your account go to Purchases > Click the Order # > Get Support, so we can make Looper better together.

If you feel that Looper is useful to you, please give your best words in the comment page and give it high stars. It is very helpful for future development.
